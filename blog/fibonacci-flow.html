Fibonacci Flow — Spirals in Motion

By Christopher Vaught • Oct 2025 • 6 min read

The Fibonacci sequence shows up everywhere — in pinecones, nautilus shells, seed heads, and galaxies. This project turns those numbers into motion: squares accumulate, arcs connect, and a spiral unfurls with each step.

In the first prototype, I placed squares on a growing grid and animated bezier arcs between their corners. The remix smoothed camera motion and applied gentle hue drift to suggest depth. Audio hooks let the rhythm lock to growth steps for a quiet, meditative pace.

Small variations create whole new families of patterns: tweak the golden angle, add easing to radius growth, or modulate hue per step — the spiral changes character while staying rooted in the same math.
